What we offer Create, build and grow your business

The SRP International team will be with you for every stage of your investment journey. From your initial interest, we will help you understand the operating environment, incorporate your company, manage your financial & regulatory reporting, grow your business through digital means, and manage your physical assets. Rely upon us to secure your investment and help you prosper.

The Company Secretarial team will incorporate your company in Sri Lanka, UAE, UK or Singapore. Thereafter, they will be responsible for corporate governance to keep your investment secure.

The Financial Services team offers a technology based solution to your accountancy needs. Monthly management reporting, invoicing, cash management and the preparation of financial statements are core services.

The Research and Business Panning comprises two distinct teams, both providing the business owner the clarity to make informed investment decisions. Our Business Planning Unit prepares bespoke research reports, feasibility studies, business plans and lead generation services. Our Equity Research team offers cost-effective portfolio analysis via ongoing tracking and reporting of publicly traded stocks.

The Human Resource Management team can assist in your handling of your most important asset, your staff. Services include recruitment, writing tailored Company HR policies, staff contracts, monthly payroll execution, social security calculation and dispute resolution.
