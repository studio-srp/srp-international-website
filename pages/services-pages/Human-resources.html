How do I hire and manage personnel of my business

SRP Human Resource Management offers a solution to handling your staff effectively. By assisting in the recruitment, documentation, review and payroll processes, we can manage a high intensity component of your company.

Employing and managing personnel is key to the growth of your firm. The right hires are needed to ensure your vision and goals are achievable. SRP International Human Resource Management is well-placed to address your specific recruitment needs, drafting HR documentation such as contracts and policies, providing payroll services, helping Management to review KPIs & personal development plans and assisting in the handling of industrial disputes.
