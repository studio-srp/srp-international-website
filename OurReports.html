Why should you refer to our reports?

SRP International Research provides relevant and current information to our clients. Every month we publish the Financial and Economic Analysis of the Sri Lankan Economy, focused on inward bound investment. Further, SRP International Research publishes a series of authoritative Sector Reports on the key areas of the local economy. These reports provide an in-depth resource for any potential investor.

Beyond the broader macro and geopolitical analysis, we can respond quickly to client requests for specific information and reporting.
