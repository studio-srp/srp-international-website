Ceylon Productions + Silk Road Partners

Ceylon Productions, founded in 2011, is a timeless boutique in the streets of Galle that offers authentic travel poster art and fine prints to transport you into a different era with their vintage aesthetic element. As a client of our Essential Accountancy service, we assisted in their financial onboarding process by integrating their real-time sales data with Xero, the online accounting system used by Silk Road. Our team integrated Hike – the point of sales system currently used by Ceylon Productions – with Xero, to ensure real-time sales, customer, and inventory data is recorded on both systems. This integration has allowed both Ceylon Productions and Silk Road Financial Services to go paperless in the accounting process. Ceylon Productions is also engaged with Silk Road’s Company Secretarial function for governance and corporate administration.

Shakticola + Silk Road Partners

Shakticola (Pvt) Ltd, established in 2012 is an architectural design & construction project management company based in Southern Sri Lanka. Their portfolio includes luxury villas, hotels, and resorts.

Silk Road Human Resource Management was engaged to recruit a technical draftsman for the company. The ideal candidate needed to have an in-depth understanding of specialised design software that are unique to the company as well as be well versed in IT. The biggest challenge of the case was that this is a niche specialisation in the industry and that there is a shortage of talent in this field who would be willing to work for a company based on the southern coast of Sri Lanka.

Our team drafted an appropriate job advertisement, reached out to suitable candidates, conducted interviews to shortlist the candidates, and sent them across to the client. The client along with Silk Road’s expertise was able to hire the ideal candidate to fill this vacancy.

Shakticola is also engaged with Silk Road’s Research & Business Planning and Company Secretarial functions for strategy, governance, and corporate administration.

Silk Road Partners Launches Sri Lanka’s First 4 Day Work Week

We are proud to announce that Silk Road Partners is Sri Lanka’s First Company to Launch a 4-Day Work Week Program

How would we continue to deliver great service to our clients whilst giving our staff a well deserved break, every week? Our staff will be working on a roster basis, with their days off either on a Monday or a Friday, ensuring that there will be no disruption in the workflow within any department of the company.

We have been investing heavily into our team’s culture, communication and collaboration skills, and facilitating them with the necessary automations to ease our transition. An emphasis was placed on prioritization, self-reflection, autonomy, and synchronization across the firm.

With technology adoption streamlining efficiency, we expect employee productivity to increase substantially, over the next couple of months. This should also translate to a reduction in the number of hours spent working overall.
